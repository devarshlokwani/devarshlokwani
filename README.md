# 💫 About Me:
I'm Devarsh Lokwani, a recent Bachelor of Information Technology graduate from Macquarie University, Sydney, where I majored in Artificial Intelligence. I'm a full-stack developer and applied-AI enthusiast who enjoys turning real-world problems into working, well-designed products.<br><br>Most recently, I worked as a Software Engineer at Robotic Marketer, where I owned the Contacts module of a fully interconnected CRM powering an AI marketing platform, built alongside a 6-person Agile team. I designed automated linking between Contacts, Accounts, and Opportunities, built persona-driven contact classification and buying-group tracking for Account-Based Marketing across B2B, B2C, eCommerce, and Education clients, and shipped full CRUD operations, bulk actions, soft delete/restore, and CSV import/export pipelines from backend to UI.<br><br>I work across the stack with TypeScript, Python, Node.js, Express, Django, and MongoDB, and I care as much about clean, intuitive user experience as I do about solid engineering. I'm currently building Foundr, a finance tracking SaaS that helps solo founders understand their burn rate, runway, ROI, and margins without needing an accounting background (live at [https://foundr-xi.vercel.app](https://foundr-xi.vercel.app)).<br><br>Outside of code, I've completed industry job simulations with Deloitte, Citi, and Amazon, and I'm always looking for the next opportunity to learn, build, and contribute to meaningful projects in AI, data, and full-stack development.
